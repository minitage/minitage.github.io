Minitage and projects templates¶

Getting cgwb up & running¶

We use cgwb to generate projects, just launch it and enjoy:: Goal is to choose a template, answer to questions and get a generated tarball from there.

./minitagatool.sh cgwb

Then naviguate to CGWB

Stop it by hitting <C-C>
